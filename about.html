Foundation & Mission

Repair Cafe in Somerville was founded in September 2022, with the mission to reduce electornic waste in the community and foster a culture of reducing waste that is essential for us to cope with the challenges of the future. We're under the umberella of the namesake international organization founded in 2010, based in Amsterdam. Since then, we have helped hundreds of people to give their beloved devices a scond life!
