Work With Us!

At Repair Cafe, we prioritize a strong work-life balance for all of our employees. We strive to create an inclusive, yet fast-paced atmosphere that suits the strengths of all workers participating in our assistive efforts for our customers.

We believe that collaboration is key, and each employee's strengths can be combined in order to solve problems. Even if stuck on a certain task, there are other members around you to help and make sure that each task gets finished. We know that fixing things can be difficult, so our staff's collaborative mindset will help you when stuck on any assignment given!
