Our Toolkit

This is where Repair Cafe can help you! Instead of disposing of your item, our trained staff members can help you repair whatever is needed. Our process is very simple -- bring your damaged goods to our shop, explain the problem you are having with your item, and we will fix it! Our customer guarantee is that no item is too large, complex, or difficult for us to repair, and our promise to you is that we will not return any item brought to us until it is fully functioning again.

Why Choose Us?

For us, our cafe not only can help save the environment and help make others environmentally conscious, but more importantly, can be a lot cheaper than replacing the item in question. With our services, we can get you back with a fully functioning machine or good in a short amount of time at a much more affordable price.
